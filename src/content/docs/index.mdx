## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Cherax Wiki the official Cherax website?

No. Cherax Wiki is an unofficial, fan-made collection of tutorials written and maintained by community members from the Cherax Discord Server. For purchases and official product information, visit [cherax.menu](https://cherax.menu).

### Where do I start if I just bought Cherax?

Read [Purchasing Cherax](/purchasing/purchasing-cherax/) if you haven't bought it yet, then follow [Getting Started](/purchasing/getting-started/) to set up the menu and avoid common errors.

### Can I use Cherax on Linux?

Yes. See the [Using Cherax on Linux](/guides/using-cherax-on-linux/) guide for setup steps under Proton/Wine.

### How do I write my own Lua scripts for Cherax?

Follow the [Writing Lua Scripts](/guides/creating-luas/) guide, which walks through the ImGui and feature APIs with a working example.
