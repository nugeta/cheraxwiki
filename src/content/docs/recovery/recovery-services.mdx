## Essential Scripts

The 3 essential scripts for any recovery service provider are:

- **Silent Night** - the best recovery script on the market, mainly focuses on modded heists, safest and boasts many features. Highly suggest getting the Supporter edition as it's quite cheap and allows for you to use Pocket Dimension (explained later on)
- **Jinxscript** - Has 600+ features to assist you on your journey, stuff like Bypass LSC to be able to put any car into Los Santos Customs, and better gun van weapons for when you need weapons like the Widowmaker, Unholy Hellbringer, Railgun, Up n Atomizer etc
- **Bunnyscript** - the ultimate vehicle modding script, used for making presets so you can mod a car once, save all its colors and similar and then have those colors applied on another vehicle in a click, plus the Donator+ feature allows you to spawn and convert a whole folder of cars at once for speed and efficiency

## Pricing, Range & ETA

Let's focus on what to nail down before every recovery: pricing, range of services, and ETA.

On pricing, be reasonable - there are many people out there selling recovery services for upwards of $150 with a menu costing $50. Having much more reasonable prices attracts more customers. Your main principle should be **"customers shouldn't need to pay for my service more than my menu costs"**. The average recovery costs about $15, more or less.

As for the range, it really depends on how much you're being paid and how much time you're willing to allocate. For people who don't pay a lot, offer the usual: money, RP, outfits, vehicles, and some buildings like business or heist properties.

Same goes for ETA, charge them accordingly for how much time they want you to dedicate to the service.

For how to get modded outfits, vehicles, and other unlocks, see the [Recovery Information](/recovery/recoveries/) page - it also has YouTube videos for the easiest explanations.

## Improving Earning Speed

To reduce the amount of time you need to spend you should improve the overall earning speed with the following methods:

- If you have Cherax Premium, consider **Free Shopping** as it is a great help - it freezes your money so that you may buy an infinite amount of stuff without spending a dime, even if you don't have enough
- Consider doing the **12 mil bonus** available on the apartment heists once daily as with custom cuts you will be able to skip a lot of the work (12 mil + max cut of 3 million = 15 million daily and safely)
- For other heists, you want to use the **teleporter** often, it's a great help for getting from your location to the heist building, then to the planning board
- Consider writing down the time you insta-finished every heist so you know when your cooldown (15-20 min) is over so you don't forget about it
- For enhanced edition clients, use **"Unlock Career Progress"** in the recovery menu - see [Recovery Information](/recovery/recoveries/) for details on what this unlocks

## Pocket Dimension

Now let's talk about the Pocket Dimension - it is a newly added feature to Silent Night which allows players to store a massive amount of money in their hangar cargo, we're talking billions.

Why do people do it? Well, think of it as a safe - anytime you need money for yourself, go to your hangar's PC, start a cargo job for about 2 million and cash out.

How does it work? You go to Silent Night, then Business Tool, then Hangar and turn it on, after which you can choose stuff such as the limit of cargo or the value of it, then how much delay should be between refills (the lower the faster but may cause instability) and other stuff like step limit and cargo type (usually animal materials by default).

## Buildings to Buy

### For Heists

- **Arcade** - Diamond Casino, speak to Lester on the map, skip cutscene in Silent Night and buy it, max payout 3.6 mil
- **Kosatka** - Cayo Perico, speak to Martin at the one location then at the Music Locker, max payout 2.55 mil
- **Facility** - Doomsday, buyable directly, use Bogdan Problem instead of Doomsday Scenario to prevent infinite loading screen, max payout 2.55 mil
- **High-End Apartment** - Apartment heist, takes a bit for Lester to call you back so rather go to your apartment after speaking to him at the garment factory and then use "Apply and Complete Preps", max payout 3 mil with no cooldown or 15 mil with the bonus, 1 hour cooldown
- **Agency** - whilst not a heist, the Dr. Dre mission has a max payout of 2.5 mil

### For Business

- **Nightclub** - best paying for legit players
- **Clubhouse** - for starting a motorcycle club
- **Bunker** - in recovery you can unlock all bunker research so you can get custom bullet types
- **Auto Shop** - allows you to earn a few million to the side with the menu
- **Other buildings** - stuff like the counterfeit cash businesses and weed shops are all optional, their setups can be skipped with the button (in recovery)

## What Actually Risks a Ban

The only things that will risk a ban for you or your client in this game is **money**, which means you'll be safe if you set a custom RP level, do most unlocks, or cosmetic stuff like trophies in the business buildings or cash piles in the office.
