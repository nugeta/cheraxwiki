## Overview

Becoming an official Cherax reseller lets you sell Cherax licenses through your own store or platform and keep a cut of every sale. The more keys you purchase in bulk, the higher your discount - and the more you earn per key sold.

## How to Apply

Contact **ImCytox** on Discord and provide the following information:

- **Shop/Business Name** - the name of your store or brand
- **Reselling Experience** - how long you've been reselling products
- **Reselling Platforms** - which platforms you sell on, plus a link to your store or website if available
- **Payment Methods** - what payment methods you accept from your customers

## Dashboard & Verification

Once approved, you'll register an account on the [Cherax reseller dashboard](https://cherax.menu/login), which gives you:

- Tracking of key status and expiration dates
- The ability to revoke keys if needed
- Your store listed on the official [Cherax purchase page](https://cherax.menu/landing/purchase)
- Recognition as a verified Cherax reseller

## Important Notes

- **Payments:** All purchases from Cherax are accepted in **cryptocurrency only**. Terms for fronted keys (paying after you've already sold them) are negotiable.
- **Product Overview:** For a full breakdown of all Cherax products, features, and updates, visit the [official Cherax website](https://cherax.menu).
